Learn a little bit about me!

Hello, my name is Josiah Bennett and I love technology! I recently graduated from the University of Pittsburgh at Bradford with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Information Systems and Technology. In my free time I experiment with different technologies. One of my favorite projects that I maintain is a Windows Server 2008 R2 domain. Every computer in our house is connected and managed through my domain. Within my domain I also run my own DNS and DHCP servers. I have also looked into the world of VPN setup. When I'm not working on a technology project I am usually practicing my photography craft. I hope this gives you a very quick glimpse into who I am.
